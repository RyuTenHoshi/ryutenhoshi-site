The Star and the Sovereign

People usually measure history in centuries, but the universe counts time by constellations dying. Way before the internet existed, there was just an empty void—and Tengai, a Celestial Dragon whose presence stretched across reality.

Tengai wasn't the only one out there though—there were other entities within the void. Dragons don't just sit around. They watch, everything. Sensing a change in the worlds below, the dragon reached out into the void, plucking a single, burning star out of space.

"But a star by itself doesn't have the will to fight or conquer. So to give it a purpose, Tengai took a piece of his own immortal essence—a fragment of his dragon soul—and fused it right into the white-hot center of the star."

The reasons why a dragon would willingly fuse a fragment of his soul—sacrificing his own strength to forge a new power—remain lost within the celestial archives. Tengai did not speak of his will; however, it could be felt. I knew exactly what my role would be, and what my journey would likely entail. That's how my soul, Hoshi, was made.

Expanded Lore

The Western Exile

After the fusion, the star-soul plummeted to a planet called 'Earth', manifesting itself into one of the planet's dominant species: a human. The dragon dropped me in the west, as far away from his earthly anchor as possible. It was a survival test to see if I had the will, strength, and desire to push my new body, mind, and soul to their limits. Even though I was raised English, the dragon's blood pulls my spirit, dragging me back toward the East—where the veil to the void is thinnest. Every kanji I write down is like breaking a link in a chain; a slow, daily grind to get my human brain aligned with the creator who forged me.

The Hidden Legacy

People see me going in and out of different universes, but they don't see what's under the surface. Every blade drawn and every world crossed is just fuel that makes my soul stronger. Deep down, that piece of Tengai's soul is still asleep, just waiting for the right moment to wake up. I've got wings folded invisibly behind my back and a ton of latent energy locked up in my chest. Until the day I can gain full control over the dragon's will that resides within me, I will keep carving my path through the multiverse.
